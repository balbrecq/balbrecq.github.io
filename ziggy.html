Zachary Morquecho

"Everyone calls me Ziggy. When I was 19, I moved to Bakersfield, California, where I realized I had found my medium the second I moved molten glass during an intro-class. This newfound passion led to me setting up and tearing down a bench on an apartment patio just to get some torch time. The next year, I moved to North Dakota where I started working full time to pay for a home studio and glass to waste... I mean, self-teach. After two years, I realized I was working too much to pay for this studio, and it was going unused; so I walked out of that situation to follow my dream. I moved back to my hometown of Santa Clarita, California and within one year, I was blowing glass live on Melrose and in-class learning from some of the very people who piqued my interest in glass art. I am always trying to make the best of the opportunities given to me and making some beautiful glass in the process."
